Arrival in Japan 04 Sep 2018

Japan

Landing

When I arrive in Japan I was totally lost. Indeed, the fist step was to get my visa validated and the reach the group of student that were going to my university. At this point it was very difficult to discover the Japanese writting system, Kanji's, Hiragana and Katakana were all surrounding us. Except the lost of my luggage by my flight company I grabbed my bust ticket.

Bus Time !

On my way from Narita to MAchida (3h30 on bus) I took advantage of the travelling time to rest a little from the long flight and enjoy the view of the Japanese highways and mainly rural landscape

Arrival in the dormitory

Our bus finally reach Machida which is the closest stop from Aoyama Gakuin University 青山学院大学(Sagamihara Campus) but we will discover the secrets of this city in another chapter ;). To conclude this chapter, I just install myself in the dorm and start to be familiar with the train system in japan after starting my exchange student orientation Program plannned by my university.
